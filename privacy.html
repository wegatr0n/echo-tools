Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

All of our tools run entirely in your browser using WebAssembly and JavaScript. When you compress an image or merge PDFs, the files never leave your device. We do not upload your files to any server.

We use free analytics provided by Cloudflare to understand aggregated visitor trends. This data includes information such as page views, referrer URL and broad geographic region. We do not collect personally identifiable information.
